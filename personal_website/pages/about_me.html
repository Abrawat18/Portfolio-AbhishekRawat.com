About Me


I am a master student at Concordia University majoring in Software Engineering. With two career goals “Work with smart people so you learn from each other” and “Work to make products which makes you feel proud.” I started my undergraduate studies in Information Technology from Devi Ahilya University, one of the prime universities of India. My academic curriculum provided me with a preliminary knowledge in diverse field of computer science. The curiosity hence developed, has motivated me to enhance my core computing skills and knowledge, particularly in web technologies, software engineering, software design, mobile Interfaces and human computer interaction. My long term career objective is to become a software professional and get involved in the areas of my interest such as software design, mobile interfaces and software development. My ambition is to use my skills to innovate and bridge the gap between the physical human world and the computer world.
